## DIN Rail Sockets (x2)

### Definition
220V sockets mounted on the DIN rail inside the cabinet to power devices.  

A DIN rail socket is a mounting base for electrical components that allows you to snap devices into a DIN rail in an industrial control cabinet.  

### Functions
- Provides a secure, standardized, and modular way to install devices  
- Suitable for relays, timers, and other electrical components  
- Ensures neat organization and easy integration inside the cabinet
