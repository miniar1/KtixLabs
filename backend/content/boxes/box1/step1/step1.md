# MODULE 1: Electrical Cabinet of an Industrial Robot

An industrial electrical cabinet is designed to group, protect, and centralize control, distribution, monitoring, and automation equipment for electrical installations in an industrial environment.

## Main Functions
- **Power distribution**: via circuit breakers, terminal blocks  
- **Control**: buttons, relays  
- **Protection**: circuit breakers, fuses, grounding  
- **Human-Machine Interface (HMI)**: indicator lights, emergency stop buttons  

---

## 1. Plastic Enclosure Electrical Cabinet (800x600x260)

**Main function**: Protect electrical and electronic equipment (circuit breakers, contactors, relays, terminal blocks, fuses, etc.) from external hazards such as:
- Dust  
- Moisture  
- Accidental contact
