## Cable Ducts

### Definition
A cable duct is a conduit (usually plastic or metal) used to organize, protect, and neatly route cables in a cabinet, improving safety, maintenance, and aesthetics.

### Types Used
- **40x40 mm** (4 m)  
- **25x40 mm** (1 m)  

### Functions
- **Cable organization** → separating circuits (power, control, signal)  
- **Mechanical protection** → prevent wire damage  
- **Safety** → reduce risk of short circuits or accidental contact  
- **Easier maintenance** → quick identification and replacement of cables
