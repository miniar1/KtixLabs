## Harting Connectors & Cable Glands

---

### 1. 32-Pin Harting Connectors

#### Definition
A **32-pin connector** is a multipoint connector that groups up to 32 wires into a single plug.

#### Benefits
- Quick connection between the cabinet and motors or actuators  
- Easier maintenance → allows testing without disassembling the entire wiring  
- Reduces wiring errors  

---

### 2. Cable Glands PG29, PG16

#### Definition
Cable glands are mounted on the cabinet wall to secure and protect cables entering the cabinet.

#### Functions
- **Mechanical security** → firmly holds the cable in place  
- **Sealing** → prevents dust and moisture entry  
- **Protection** → safeguards cable insulation from sharp edges  
- **Safety** → maintains electrical safety and cabinet IP rating  
- **Guidance** → directs cable entry to terminal blocks neatly
