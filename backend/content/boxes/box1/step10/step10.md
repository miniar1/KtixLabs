## Fan 120mm 220V IP54 (x2)

### Definition
An electrical cabinet fan is a device that circulates air inside the cabinet to cool electrical and electronic components.

---

### Cooling Components
- Electrical equipment generates heat during operation  
- The fan expels hot air and brings in fresh air  
- Maintains optimal operating temperature  

---

### Preventing Failures
- Excessive heat can reduce component lifespan  
- Can cause malfunctions  

---

### Improving Safety
- An overheated cabinet can pose fire risks  
- The fan helps dissipate dangerous heat  

---

### Maintaining Performance
- Servo drives and motors controlled by the cabinet work better at stable temperatures  
- Prevents thermal limitation or automatic shutdown  

---

### IP54 Protection
- **IP (Ingress Protection)** rating defines resistance to dust and water  
- **5** → protects against dust  
- **4** → protects against water splashing
