## Fuses / DIN Rail Fuse Holders

### Fuse
- An electrical safety device whose main role is to **protect electrical circuits against overcurrent**.  
- Operates by breaking the circuit when current exceeds a safe threshold, preventing overheating, fire, or equipment damage.  

---

### DIN Rail Fuse Holder
- A support that allows safe installation and replacement of fuses.  
- Provides a **secure, standardized, and modular mounting** inside the cabinet.  
- Facilitates quick maintenance and replacement without rewiring.
