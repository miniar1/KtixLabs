# Cable Fuse Holder
For each wire of the cutter, attach a wire ferrule (#)

-Cut 430 mm of 1.5 mm² red wire (#) to connect the Terminal distributor box 1 (#) with the Fuse holder (repeat 4 times to get 4 wires)

-Cut 130mm of 1.5mm² red wire to connect the Fuse holder with the Din rail socket 1 (#)

-Cut 350mm of 1.5 mm² blue wire (#) to connect the Din rail socket (1 or 2??) with the Terminal distributor box 1 (#)

-Cut 1250 mm of 1.5mm² yellow wire (#) to connect the Din rail socket1 with the Ground Bar (#)

-Cut 400 mm of 1.5mm² red wire to connect the Fuse holder with the Din rail socket2 (#)

-Cut 250 mm of 1.5 mm² blue wire to connect the Din rail socket with the Terminal distributor box 1 (#)

-Cut 950mm of 1.5mm² yellow wire to connect the Din rail socket2 with the Ground Bar
